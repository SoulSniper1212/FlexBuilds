Rent to Own Program

FlexBuilds offers flexible rent-to-own options for our custom-built PCs. Pay over time with no interest!

Choose the PC you want, pay a small deposit, and rent it for 12 or 24 months. At the end of the term, you can choose to buy it or return it for an upgrade!
